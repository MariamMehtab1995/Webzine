The Power of Chess Tactics

Chess tactics are short-term moves that create immediate advantages.

Some essential tactics include:

  • Fork: One piece attacks two or more enemy pieces at once.
  • Pin: Attacking a piece so moving it would expose a more valuable piece behind it.
  • Skewer: Like a pin but attacking the more valuable piece first.
  • Discovered Attack: Moving one piece reveals an attack from another piece.

Try spotting these tactics in your games! They can turn the tide instantly.
