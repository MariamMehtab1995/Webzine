The Beauty of Chess Endgames

Endgames are quiet yet fascinating battles:

  • Lucena Position: A famous rook and pawn endgame that teaches winning technique.
  • Fortress: Defensive setups that can save a losing player.
  • Theoretical Draws: Positions known to be a draw with perfect play.

Understanding endgames is the mark of true chess mastery.
