The Psychological Game of Chess

Chess is as much mental as tactical:

  • Intuition: Experienced players often “feel” the right move without calculating every step.
  • Focus: Staying calm under pressure is key to winning tense games.
  • Mental mistakes: Even champions blunder when distracted or tired.

Managing emotions and mindset can change the outcome of tough matches.
